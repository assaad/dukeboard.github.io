--- title: EnTiMid on its way to Kevoree layout: post published: true --- EnTiMid have been designed to be a component based framework to ease both design and use of home automation solutions.
Proposed in several projects, presented in many public interventions, EnTiMid is soon to be released as a commercial product.

On its way to its industrial life, EnTiMid met Kevoree, and decided to work with.
At this time, all integrations we have done to make EnTiMid work using Kevoree brought promising good news, and new features.
