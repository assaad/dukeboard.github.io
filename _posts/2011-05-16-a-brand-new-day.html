Today is a brand new day. At least for the Kevoree familly. Today, a new bread of technology is born. 

The Kevoree team is very please to anounce you the born of 

K-Strip

the Kevoree-powered power strip. 

[[posterous-content:gdeczwGetodBuEzgenHc]]

Features

  • A status led in the front to provide users with feedback
  • A Infrared receiver to control the K-Strip using a remote control
  • 4 managed power socket with 4 status led on the top, making it possible to activate/disactivate plugged devices remotely
  • A current sensor inside, to get informed of the overall energy consuption of the K-Strip
  • Temperature and Light sensors on the top
  • A buzzer
  • A standard ElectronicBrick connector on the side to allow future user specific extentions
  • A 433Mhz Transceiver to remotely control the K-Strip using radio frequency communications
  • Firmware customizable through a USB connection.

K-Strip applications are just limited by your imagination. 

Control up to 5 DMX-compatible products(DMX extention required), while playing a song on the buzzer.
Create a lightning sequence to control four light.
Control by any mean: being placed behind your entertainment devices (TV, DVD player, Sound system), switch on and off the connected devices using a generic remote control, a radio control command.
